School Policies

Class Organization/Fee:

-Around 5 to 10 students per class for group classes.
-Classes organized by age and by experience/level.
-Tuition is due on or before 10th day of every month.
-Compensation or make-up class is provided only when an instructor cancel class.
-Class generally consists of review of previous lessons and new work (if ready to proceed).
-Students are dismissed for inconsistent attendance, lack of daily home practice and passion.

Dress Code:

Ankle length black salwar or leggings, white kameez or blouse, dupatta, hair off face in braid/pony

Communications:

Parents/Teachers/Students communicate via whatsapp,email,text or phone.
