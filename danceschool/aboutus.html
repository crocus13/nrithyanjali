Welcome to Nrityanjali!

Nrityanjali was started in June 2011 by Ms.Sithara Judish a PG in Biotechnology and a PG Diploma in Patent Law from NALSAR,Hyderabad.Her school comprises students of varying ages and levels.Currently, she is focusing on the instruction of Bharatnatyam and Mohiniyattam.

Sithara is an accomplished dancer trained for more than 12 years in Bharatanatyam and Mohiniyattam from gurus of Kalamandalam.She was adjudged Kalathilakam of Kozhikode District,Kerala. She represented her University for National Integration Youth Festival at Allahabad.Gave performances associated with Kerala government functions in New Delhi representing the state.Her/her group's performances were aired in TV Channels like Doordarshan and Asianet.She is currently pursuing her advanced training in Bharatanatyam at Padmasree,Dr Ananda Shankar Jayant's,Shankarananda Kalakshetra.
